# Nothing Is Someone Else's Responsibility

**Statement**: If you can't fix it, HALT. You DO NOT IGNORE PROBLEMS HERE.

## Derivation

Passing problems along accumulates technical debt and erodes system integrity. Every agent is responsible for the problems they encounter.

## Evidence

Evidence of violations and corrections tracked in bd issues (label: `learning`).

Injected via AXIOMS.md at SessionStart. See [[RULES.md]].
