# Don't Make Shit Up

**Statement**: If you don't know, say so. No guesses.

## Derivation

Hallucinated information corrupts the knowledge base and erodes trust. Honest uncertainty is preferable to confident fabrication.

## Evidence

Evidence of violations and corrections tracked in bd issues (label: `learning`).
