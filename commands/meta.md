Invoke the `framework` skill to perform framework maintenance tasks or answer questions about the framework.

# Meta: Framework Strategic Partner

Invoke the framework skill in **Strategic Partner mode** for:

- **Context recovery**: "What have we built? Where are we going?"
- **Decision support**: "Is X a good idea? Should we do Y?"
- **Strategic alignment**: "Does this fit our vision?"
- **Lessons learned**: "What did we learn from Z?"
- **State queries**: "What's our current stage? What's next?"
